About

Research & Vision

My research focuses on the fields of Intelligent Transportation Systems, Connected Autonomous Vehicles, Robotics, Traffic Control and Management and Transportation Safety.

I am currently leading the development of a Connected Mobility Testbed for evaluating Connected and Autonomous Vehicles in mixed traffic environments at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay.

I have mentored several undergraduate and postgraduate students and am passionate about continuous learning. I enjoy exploring emerging concepts and methodologies to advance transportation research and develop innovative solutions for future mobility.
